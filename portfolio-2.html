E-Commerce Website - Early Wireframes





Skills:


Wire-framing, Prototyping, Product Design, User Research, Figma, Auto Layout


Project:


A major design project that is currently in the design phase is the overhaul of UCLA's E-Commerce websites. The new E-Commerce experience will be powered by Magento, and will combine various current platforms into a uniform, modern E-Commerce experience.




Donation Page



This is an early wireframe that displays the components of a giving page. The design combines two current UCLA sites. "Giveto UCLA" sites currently contain the call to action for giving to a specific cause, the actual donations are made in a separate page on giving.ucla.edu. The design above combines both into one central giving experience. Working on early wireframes has helped me to master auto layout in Figma.







Donation Page - Simplified



The fundraising needs of UCLA are diverse, therefore there are multiple layouts for a donation page. This mockup is for simplified donation page which allows a donor to make a gift as quickly and painlessly as possible.







Checkout Experience



As part of the revamping of our E-Commerce ecosystem, a major priority is to modernize the checkout experience for making a donation. The design is meant to be on par with e-commerce sites such as Amazon, and incorporate modern payment methods.
