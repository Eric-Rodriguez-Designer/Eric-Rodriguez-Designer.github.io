My Work

UCLA Digital Constituent Experience

The UCLA Digital Constitent Experience Discovery Series is a series of discovery meetings with various teams within UCLA External Affairs. The purpose of these meetings is to collaborate with diverse groups in order to find ways to improve the digital experience of UCLA Constituents.

E-Commerce UCLA Sites

A major project to overhaul the E-Commerce ecosystem of UCLA Giving. Currently, UCLA E-Commerce sites consist of a combination of legacy platforms and 3rd party platforms. The goal is to unify all these platforms into a single, modern, mobile friendly giving experience. This application will be built in house with UCLA designer and developers.

Events RSVP Application

The Events RSVP Application project will replace the current legacy platform which is used to facilitate sign-ups by UCLA constituents to External Affairs hosted events. The new platform will be modern, mobile friendly and support a self-service content management system.

ATS Marketing Site

Marketing site for UCLA SAAS products. UCLA ATS has developed digital products for university advancement professionals. These products have garnered interest from other Universities that wish to licence them.

Alumni Scholars Club Application

The Alumni Scholars Club is a program run by UCLA Alumni Affairs that supports UCLA scholarship recipients. My team designed and implemented a web application to assist them in managing their popular complex program.

UCLA Auctions Application

Mobile auctionns application to support UCLA fundraising events.
