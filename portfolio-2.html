Project Overview

A major design project in the UCLA Advancement Technology Solutions portfolio is the overhaul of UCLA's E-Commerce websites. The new experience will be powered by Magento and will combine multiple existing platforms into a single, uniform, modern e-commerce experience for donors and participants.

Donation Page

This early wireframe shows the components of a unified giving page. The design combines two current UCLA sites — "GivetoUCLA" sites with the call to action, and the actual donation flow on giving.ucla.edu — into one central giving experience. Mastering Figma's Auto Layout was critical to building these at scale.

Donation Page — Simplified

UCLA's fundraising needs are diverse, so multiple layout variants are required. This mockup is for a simplified donation page that allows donors to make a gift as quickly and painlessly as possible, reducing friction in the giving flow.

Checkout Experience

A top priority for the e-commerce revamp is modernizing the checkout experience. The design brings UCLA's donation checkout on par with leading e-commerce sites and incorporates modern payment methods to remove barriers to giving.
