UCLA Digital Constituent Experience

Role UX Designer & Product Manager
Skills User Research · Web Design · Brand Management
Tools Figma · WordPress · Presentations
Back to Projects

Project Overview

Alongside the Technology Product Manager of UCLA ATS and members of the ATS Team, I led a series of focus groups called the UCLA Digital Constituent Experience Discovery Series. In this series, we met with different teams across UCLA External Affairs to collaboratively discuss ways to create a more uniform and positive digital experience for UCLA's diverse constituents.

WordPress Site Landing Page

To supplement the discovery meetings, I designed and built a marketing site hosted through WordPress. The site explained the initiative to UCLA External Affairs and provided a central reference for all our user research findings.

Discovery Series Presentation Deck

To facilitate discussion, the Technology Product Manager and I developed a presentation deck tailored to the unique needs of every individual team. The deck explained the Discovery Series goals and provided discussion prompts — all designed to conform to UCLA Brand Guidelines. To simplify future web and marketing work, we created a Figma Style Library containing all UCLA brand elements including color codes and typography.

Post-Meeting Digest

After each meeting, the Product team reviewed notes and recordings to compose a post-meeting digest summarizing all feedback. We published these documents to the website so all teams could review action points and view findings from other teams' sessions.
