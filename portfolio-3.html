Events RSVP Application





Skills:


Wire-framing, Creating High Fidelity Mock-ups, Prototyping, Product Design, User Research, Figma, User Journey Maps, Site-Mapping


Project:


In addition to gift sites, another major application offered by ATS are Event RSVP sites. These sites help connect UCLA constituents with UCLA Events put on by the External Affairs or Alumni Affairs departments. These events are a major source of engagement and donations from constituents.

The current platform is an outdated legacy platform. In my role as E-Commerce Product designer, I am responsible, for the research and design of the product.

High Fidelity Comps



Once the elements of a particular module of the product are approved by the Technology Product Manager, I create a high fidelity annoted wireframe. This may be shared with the client for approval. The mock-up also serves as a reference to our developers when building the product.









Mobile High-Fidelity Comps



Our events RSVP application was designed with a mobile first philosophy. The goal is that UCLA constiutents will be able to quickly and seamlessly sign up for events on their mobile devices.
