Project Overview

UCLA ATS built several products — including Gift Agreement Builder and Gift Hub — that generated interest from other universities looking for similar solutions. My team was tasked with designing and implementing a marketing site to promote these products as licensable software available from UCLA ATS to the broader higher-education market.

ATS Marketing Site

The site presents a catalog of completed products ready for licensing, communicating capabilities and value to prospective university partners.

Gift Agreement Builder Product Page

Each product has a dedicated detail page. The Gift Agreement Builder page showcases the product that digitally automates the process of creating gift agreement documents for UCLA donors — eliminating a previously manual, paper-based workflow.
